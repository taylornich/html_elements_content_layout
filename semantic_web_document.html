Books in the Sun Eater Series

GoodReads Link Rating Additional Comments
Empire of Silence 4/5
  • This would have been a 5 star read if I had not read The Name of the Wind beforehand, the plot is almost exactly on pace with TNotW
  • It was very easy to get invested in the characters, there werer several occasions that I teared up from the events of the book and character deaths
Howling Dark 4.5/5
  • This book moves past the world building in book one and begins the story which will ultimately lead to the ending.
  • Kharn Sagara - probably my absolute favorite side character in the series.
Demon in White 4.6/5
  • The assassination attempts
  • Events of this book are unparalleled by the rest of the series
Kingdoms of Death 4.2/5
  • I hate the Lothrian Commonwealth - leads to one of my most uncomfortable arcs in the whole series
  • Finally get to better know the cielcen
Ashes of Man 4/5
  • Valka's death was poorly written and long awaited by this book
  • Valka changes her mind about children and after she dies Hadrian has her child created??
  • The final book was split into 2 separate books, which is fine BUT this book was honesly boring in comparison to the rest.
